Whenever someone asks what my studies are good for, I think of the tree in Zhuangzi. It survived precisely because it was useless to the carpenter.

> But life is not an optimisation problem. Or if it is, we have not yet defined the objective function.

## On Leisure

Aristotle held that leisure was the foundation of civilisation. The word *school* descends from the Greek *scholē*, meaning leisure. Real learning needs blank space.
